Our Educational Services

We offer comprehensive education beyond the national curriculum, meeting international standards.

English Version Section

At Raydon Academy, our school builds a strong foundation in English for our national curriculum. Classes: Class (1 – 9) Curriculum: Aligned with international standards Subjects Offered: All subjects offered by Bangladesh National Curriculum Board.

English Medium Section

Our English Medium Section (Std 1 – Std 8) provides a strong academic base with international standards Subjects Offered: English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Social Studies, ICT, Bangla, Islamic Studies and Moral Education.

O Level (Cambridge & Edexcel)

We offer a world-class O Level program under both Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) and Pearson Edexcel boards, ensuring global recognition and academic excellence. Boards Offered: Cambridge (CAIE) & Edexcel Subjects Offered: Core: English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology. Optional: Business Studies, Accounting, ICT, Economics and Geography.

A Level (Cambridge & Edexcel)

Our A Level program (Cambridge & Edexcel) provides advanced academic training and prepares students for top universities worldwide. Classes: A Level (Grade 11 – Grade 12) Boards Offered: Cambridge (CAIE) & Pearson Edexcel Subjects Offered: Science Stream: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, ICT. Business & Humanities Stream: Business Studies, Economics, Accounting, Law, Psychology, Sociology Language & Additional: English Language, Extensive practice with past papers & timed mock exams

English Language Training

We believe that mastering English opens doors to global opportunities. Our English Language Training programs are designed to help learners of all levels - from beginners to advanced - build strong communication, writing, and comprehension skills for both academic and professional success. What We Offer - Spoken English Training: Improve fluency, pronunciation, and confidence in daily and professional conversations. Grammar & Vocabulary Mastery: Learn essential grammar rules and enrich your vocabulary for accurate and effective communication. Business English: Develop strong English skills for meetings, presentations, and workplace communication.

Academic English

Prepare for exams, essays, and international study requirements. IELTS & English Proficiency Test Preparation: Get expert guidance and practice for top scores. Why Choose Our English Training: Expert and friendly trainers with real classroom and corporate experience. Practical, interactive, and fun learning sessions. Customized lessons for children, students, and professionals. Online and in-person classes available. Affordable fees with flexible class schedules. Our goal is to help every learner speak, write, and think in English with confidence - turning language learning into a life-changing skill.

About Raydon Academy

Raydon Academy is the first fully online English Version and English Medium school in Bangladesh, offering world-class education from primary to higher levels.
We provide both National (English Version) and Cambridge Curriculum/Pearson Edexcel (English Medium) education through a smart, interactive online system designed for modern learners.
In addition to school education, Raydon Academy proudly operates as a professional English Language Training Centre, helping students, professionals, and job seekers improve their speaking, writing, and communication skills.
Our mission is to make quality education and English training accessible to everyone - anytime, anywhere. With qualified teachers, affordable fees, and global learning standards, we prepare students for academic excellence and real-world success.
